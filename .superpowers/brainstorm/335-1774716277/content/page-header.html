B: Spacious — Title block with summary stats, then filters + actions row

Breadcrumb, title, and a summary stat line ("142 items · 12 at risk") as a title block. Then a combined filters + actions row separated by a subtle divider. More breathing room, dashboard feel. Used by Procore project pages.
